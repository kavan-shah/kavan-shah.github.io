About me

Hi! I'm Kavan Shah, a Robotics and Controls Engineer from Ahmedabad, India, currently working in the U.S. manufacturing sector. Welcome to my website!

I'm passionate about advancing industrial automation through the integration of robotics, Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), and cutting-edge digital technologies.

In 2023, I earned my Master's degree in Robotics from the University of Michigan. During my time there, I conducted research with the Advanced Manufacturing Lab, focusing on developing smart manufacturing solutions that blend existing infrastructure with modern technologies. My academic journey provided me with extensive hands-on experience in robotics through various research projects and coursework.

Currently, I'm working as an Automation and Controls Engineer in Minnesota, where I develop electrical and control systems for part-finishing manufacturing machines. This role allows me to deepen my understanding of the automation industry in a practical, industry-focused setting.

My goal is to bridge the gap between traditional manufacturing processes and the future of industry, creating more efficient, flexible, and intelligent production systems. Through this website, I aim to showcase my projects, research, and the innovative solutions I've developed in the field of robotics and industrial automation.

I'm always eager to learn about new developments in the field and exchange ideas with fellow professionals, so feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find the projects and research presented here informative and inspiring.
